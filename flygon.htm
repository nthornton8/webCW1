Flygon is a dual-type Ground/Dragon Pokémon.

It evolves from Vibrava starting at level 45. It is the final form of Trapinch. It can Mega Evolve into Mega Flygon using the Flygonite. It can transform into Armored Flygon while holding the Flygon Armor.

Trivia

  • Flygon shares its species names with Xatu. They are both known as the Mystic Pokémon in English, and the Spirit Pokémon in Japanese.
  • Up until Generation VII, Flygon was the only fully-evolved Pokémon lacking a Hidden Ability that evolves from a Pokémon with a Hidden Ability. This changed with the introduction Alolan Raichu in Pokémon Sun and Moon.
  • Flygon has the same base Attack as its base form, Trapinch.
  • All of Vibrava's base stats go up by exactly 30 points when it evolves into Flygon.
  • Flygon's EV Yield (1 Attack, 2 Speed) is unique.
  • Flygon and its pre-evolution are the only Dragon-type Pokémon belonging in the Bug Egg Group.
  • Flygon is the only Pokémon which gains Bug typing when evolving or Mega Evolving.
    • Therefore it is also the only Pokémon which loses a type in favor of Bug when evolving or Mega Evolving.
    • Flygon is also the only Pokémon to lose its primary type upon Mega Evolving.
  • Mega Flygon is the only Pokémon with a base stat total of 620.
  • Mega Flygon has the highest base stat total and base Special Attack of all Bug-type Pokémon in Pokémon Insurgence.
